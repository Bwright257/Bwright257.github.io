Shattered Echo

Shattered Echo is an action role-playing game based on titles such as Dark Souls and Elden Ring. Fight your way through challenging enemies and reunite the fragments of the Echo.

Project Overview

Shattered Echo is a cooperative multiplayer soulslike inspired by many past games of the genre. I and three others in my team were tasked with developing a game as part of our senior capstone course at Edinboro University. We executed this assignment with the help of weekly agile sprints which greatly improved our workflow. During this project, I was in charge of developing all gameplay-related systems. Overall, I loved pushing my skills within Unreal Engine to their limits and achieving goals that I didn't think would be possible.

Notable Features

Immersive Combat

Like many soulslike games, combat was designed to be at the forefront of Shattered Echo. Players are thrown into a world that apparently has a singular goal: to defeat them. Multiple types of enemies are aggressive towards the players and will actively seek them out, such as the Draugr and Ravenous Beast. To combat these foes, players are able to execute melee attacks with their trusty longsword, parry enemy attacks at the moment of contact, and dodge roll out of the way of incoming attacks. At the end of each of the game's three levels, powerful enemies stand guard to prevent players from proceeding. Fyronax the Accursed, Azurael the Soul Devourer, and the Tyrant King are foes that require players to utilize every aspect of the combat system.

Cooperative Multiplayer

Players are able to host lobbies or join others using a fully integrated session system. You and up to three friends can experience Shattered Echo together and brave the many dangerous encounters you're bound to run into. The multiplayer experience remains as seamless as playing standalone, as all player and enemy animations are replicated between each client. Be wary, however, as while playing together, the world itself changes. Enemies become tougher, and their newfound allies stand guard.
