Who am I?

There are millions of game developers out there, so what makes me different? Personally I'm still finding that one out! For now though, I'd say it's my drive and determination. I've picked up dozens of new tools and software in the relatively short time that I've been creating games - and I'm always looking to learn more. My personal goal is to make things that people will love for years to come, so what better option is there than game development!

In my personal life, when I'm not creating games of course, I love travelling around the country with friends and finding new experiences. These often serve to be amazing motivators for when the journey is over and it's time to have fun making games. Also, most of my free time is also spent listening to music - which I find greatly helps with my work.
