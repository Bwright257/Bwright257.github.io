The Monolith

The Monolith is a two-dimensional platforming game with role-playing elements. Journey through the lands, seek the mysterious Monolith, and discover items that grant you mystical powers.

Project Overview

The Monolith was created as an intermediate learning assignment for a game programming class during my junior year. The specifications for this project were given all at once and executed using a waterfall methodology. Despite having a mountain of work in front of me right from the beginning, I was excited to delve into the realm of 2D game design within Unreal Engine.

Notable Features

Dynamic Combat

Going into the project, my vision was to create a game that emphasizes fun more than anything else. My combat system was developed with this idea constantly in mind. Both players and enemies have fluid and dynamic attacks that can be chained together to form unique combos. An integrated inventory system also allows the player to change their attack styles between a sword, bow, and magic spells on a whim.

World Interaction

A good player controller is often the key to having fun while playing any game. The majority of this project's development time went into refining the feel of the player and all of the possible actions a player can take. Wallrunning, climbing, and sliding abilities were created to give the player agency to explore the game world to their heart's content. With these tools, players can quickly dodge enemy attacks and find secrets hidden within levels that would otherwise be unreachable.
